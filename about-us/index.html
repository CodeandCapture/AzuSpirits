About Azu Spirits

Where Industry Expertise Meets Passion for Exceptional Spirits

Our Story

Founded by Ellie Asthana Gibson, Azu Spirits began with a simple passion: a love for single malt whisky and a desire to share exceptional spirits in a more engaging, personal way. What started as a personal blog exploring the production and science behind spirits has evolved into a carefully curated online shop celebrating quality, diversity, and the rich stories behind every bottle.

Since launching in 2021, we've built a reputation for expertise and thoughtful curation. Our collections reflect a deep understanding of spirits—from the subtle regional variations in Scottish single malts to the innovative craft gin movement sweeping Britain.

A Journey Through the Industry

  • Working in Glasgow's top whisky bars, immersed in Scotland's liquid heritage
  • Showcasing premium brands at whisky festivals across the UK
  • Crafting cocktails at 1806 in Melbourne, one of the world's most celebrated cocktail bars
  • Researching distillation methods and the science of flavour development
  • Building direct relationships with craft distilleries across Britain

Our Philosophy

Every spirit in our collection has been personally selected based on quality, character, and story. We champion craft producers, celebrate regional diversity, and seek out bottles that showcase the artistry and science of distillation. Whether you're exploring single malt whisky or discovering craft gin, we're here to guide and inspire your journey.

Why Choose Azu Spirits

Our shop isn't just about selling spirits—it's about sharing the knowledge, passion, and expertise gained from years in hospitality and the whisky industry. We understand what makes a spirit truly special.

Industry Expertise

From understanding the impact of cask types on whisky maturation to recognising the botanical signatures that define exceptional gin, our selections are informed by genuine expertise and hands-on experience in bars, festivals, and tasting rooms. We taste, research, and curate with care.

Rich Diversity & History

Our collections celebrate the incredible range within each spirit category—from delicate Lowland whiskies to robust Islays, from floral London Dry gins to bold contemporary styles. These products illustrate the rich diversity, history, and science we've discovered throughout our journey in spirits.

Science Meets Craft

What began as research into production processes and the science behind spirits continues to inform everything we do. We don't just sell bottles—we share the fascinating stories of how terroir, botanicals, fermentation, distillation, and maturation create the flavours you love.

Thoughtfully Curated

Our blind tasting gift sets and themed collections offer unique ways to explore spirits. Designed for discovery and comparison, they're perfect for enthusiasts and newcomers alike, bringing the professional tasting experience into your home.

We also offer bespoke tasting events and mixology experiences for special occasions. Get in touch to learn more.

What Sets Our Shop Apart

Curated Single Malts

Discover Scotland's diverse whisky regions through our carefully selected single malts. From the gentle, floral character of Speyside to the bold, peaty expressions of Islay, each bottle represents years of craft and tradition.

Regional Craft Gins

Explore Britain's gin renaissance with our regional collections. From Devon's innovative distilleries to Yorkshire's craft producers, we showcase the quality, creativity, and distinctive botanicals that define modern British gin.
