The Six Single Malts

Take a tour through the Scottish regions with six premium single malts

Speyside

Glenfiddich Solera 15

Soft sweet sherry

15-year-old whiskies from Oloroso sherry, bourbon and virgin oak casks are married together in a Solera Vat that has never been emptied. The complex layers of sherry and spice compliment Glenfiddichs unpeated and soft spirit.

Highlands

Dalmore 12

Rich chocolate & orange

Matured in American oak bourbon casks before being transferred to Oloroso sherry, this is sweet and rich. A full-bodied Highlander with classic chocolate and orange Dalmore flavours.

Campbeltown

Glen Scotia 15

Spicy and rich

Unpeated and matured in a combination of first fill and re-fill American Oak casks, this is vanilla forward with spicy aromatic fruits.

Isle of Mull

Ledaig 10

Smoky, vanilla. & maritime

The peated expression from the Tobermory distillery on the Isle of Mull. An underlying coastal character, light to medium bodied spirit with powerful peat smoke.

Lowland

Bladnoch Vinaya

Grassy and floral

Bladnoch is the most southerly distillery in Scotland. Matured in first fill bourbon and first fill sherry casks, this is floral with a bold oak spice.

Islay

Kilchoman Machir Bay

Fruits and smoke

Warm peat smoke, citrus and tropical fruits. Spice, cracked black pepper and sherried fruits. Matured in Oloroso sherry butts and American oak bourbon barrels.
