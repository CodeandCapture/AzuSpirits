The Scottish Tasting Tour

Six new single malts. A whisky-lover's route through Scotland. One mystery collection — and you can be the very first to taste what we find.

Get First Access

This August, the Azu Spirits family is heading to the heart and home of Scotch whisky. Tasked with finding six new single malt whiskies for an exclusive themed tasting collection, we'll be revisiting some of our very favourite distilleries — exploring new tastes, learning from the masters, and discovering truly Scottish stays along the way.

The Route

From Highland Perthshire to the peat fires of the Hebrides — we'll be visiting distilleries and whisky bars to find the very best variety

1

Aberfeldy

Highland Perthshire

The journey begins among the hills and barley fields of the central Highlands, where Scotch whisky's heartland traditions run deepest.

2

Oban

Gateway to the Isles

The seafood capital of Scotland and our crossing point to the Hebrides — a harbour town built around its whisky.

3

Isle of Mull

The Islands

Across the water to Mull's painted harbourfront and island drams shaped by sea air and Hebridean weather.

4

Islay

The Whisky Isle

Whisky's most famous island — home of the boldest, peatiest expressions in all of Scotch.

5

Glasgow

Where it all began

Ellie's love for Scotch whisky was first inspired by her years living in Glasgow — working in renowned whisky bars and visiting distilleries on weeks off. The tour ends back where it all began.

Our Most Exciting Chapter Yet

Our first Scottish tour in 2019 inspired the start of Azu Spirits — and everything we've built since: a line of themed blind tasting experiences that showcase the diversity of flavour in Scotch, share the stories behind each distillery, and reveal how craft and technique shape every dram.

After years spent perfecting the blind tasting format, hosting tastings and events, and learning what makes a dram memorable, this trip marks the start of our most exciting chapter yet. The collection we bring home will headline a Christmas season full of new launches — including something we've never made before — and lay the foundations for taking the Azu Spirits experience beyond our online home in the year ahead.

If you're in the Scotch whisky trade and our paths could cross around the first week of August — we'd love to hear from you.
