What's in the box?

Papillon Original Gin
A complex yet delicate gin distilled from 17 botanicals, including juniper, angelica, coriander, gorse, hawthorn berries, and Devon violets. A unique floral character created using Dartmoor spring water and an overnight steeping process in the copper pot still.

Dartmouth Gin
A well-balanced, aromatic gin with citrus, spice, floral notes, and sweetness. Distilled with precision using a Müller copper pot still and a vapour infusion chamber to preserve delicate flavours.

Brixham Gin Tattered Sails Hopped IPA Release
A gin inspired by a traditional IPA, with a unique blend of citra hops, juniper, and bergamot lemon. Crafted by the family-owned Brixham Distillery, reflecting the maritime heritage of the picturesque town.

Copper Frog Gin
A smooth gin with layers of complexity, created using pure filtered water from Exmoor National Park. Distilled in a handmade Portuguese still, Copper Frog's locally renowned gin offers delicate and fragrant aromas.

Thunderflower Gin
A smooth, spicy, and herbaceous gin crafted using Dartmoor spring water and vapour infusion. Featuring botanicals like juniper, coriander, cassia bark, pink peppercorns, cardamom, and earthy notes of sage, elderflower, and lemongrass.

Exeter Gin London Dry
Bold and aromatic with bursts of citrus and spice, Exeter Gin incorporates basil, cinnamon, cardamom, and marigold, alongside 11 other botanicals. A tribute to the city's Roman heritage, produced with a strong family legacy.
