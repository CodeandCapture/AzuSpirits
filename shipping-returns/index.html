Where we deliver

Our products are available to deliver to addresses in the British Isles, including mainland UK, Guernsey, Isle of Man, Isle of Wight, Jersey, Northern Ireland, Scottish Highlands and Scottish Islands.

Shipping times

We have a 1 - 2 day handling time and use DPD Next Day service for reliable shipping.

We will provide a tracking link for your parcel, in the unlikely event the shipment is lost during transit we will send replacement or a full refund.

Returns

If you would like to return your order, please email us with the reason for the return. We will send a return label and issue a refund once we have received the item. In the unlikely event that your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days of receiving the item, with proof of damage. We will send a replacement free of charge.
