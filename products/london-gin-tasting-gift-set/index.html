The Six London Gins

A taste of the Capital

Unique Botanicals

Half Hitch Gin

Half Hitch Gin, using 100% British grain spirit, uses three production methods to extract the optimal flavour from their botanicals: Copper pot distillation, vacuum distillation and hand-crafted tinctures.

Sweeter Old Tom

Sacred Old Tom Gin

A high-strength interpretation of the 18th-century Old Tom style. With bold juniper and sweet orange peel, the distillery uses a high proportion of distilled liquorice to attain a natural sweetness.

Small Batch

Little Bird London Dry

Handcrafted in micro batches in London, Little Bird’s blend of pink grapefruit, sweet orange peel and ginger brings crisp citrus notes whilst seven more botanicals add to the smooth, fresh flavour.

Herbaceous & Spicy

Doghouse Renegade Gin

A distinct, herbaceous, earthy and spicy gin. Uses green, leafy botanicals such as bay, sage, winter savoury and cardamom alongside traditional gin botanicals.

Traditional Dry

Jensens Bermondsey Gin

A traditional London dry gin paying homage to the quality spirits produced in mid-20th-century London. Designed to be the perfect spirit in a martini, balanced and not too dry.

Popular release

Portobello Road No 171 Gin

Rich juniper with a fresh citrus character. Liquorice and nutmeg promote the sweet peppery finish. Using natural botanicals from around the world to create a unique profile and depth of flavour, Portobello Road has quickly become the gin of Notting Hill.
